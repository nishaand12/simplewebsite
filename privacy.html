Cookie Policy

A cookie is a small file, stored on a user's hard drive by a website. It's purpose is to collect data relating to the user's browsing habits. You can choose to be notified each time a cookie is transmitted. You can also choose to disable cookies entirely in your internet browser, but this may decrease the quality of your user experience. We use the following types of cookies on our site:

  • Functional cookies: These are cookies that are required for the proper functioning of the site. They do not collect personal data.
  • Performance cookies: These are cookies that are used to collect information about how you use our Site, for example data on the content you access, how long you stay on our Site, etc.
